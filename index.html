About Me

Hi, my name is Pat and I have many hobbies. Some are creative, some are dangerous, and some are relaxing. I also love my job. I work as a database developer, data engineer, data warehouse engineer....you get the idea. I am crrently attending a full-stack web development bootcamp through UC Berkeley. You ask "Why would you do that if you love your job?". Well, my job has changed a lot over the years and it is not sufficient to have only data skills. Many data professionals now write APIs, scripts, and programs using object-oriented languages. They may also be required to hook up a website to a database. I believe that full-stack web development skills will help my career enormously.
